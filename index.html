About Us

MIT Asian Dance Team (ADT) is a student organization established in 2009 to spread the culture of East Asian dance, from traditional Asian dance to modern Asian pop, from ethnic pieces to contemporary performances. ADT is currently the largest dance organization on MIT campus, with over 250 members consisting of MIT students and affiliates.

Join

ADT members learn dances for performance in a biannual showcase open to the general public. In addition to these showcases, ADT also performs at various events within the MIT and Boston community. Members of ADT can optionally also participate in ADT dance cover videos.

To get updated when ADT auditions or workshops are happening, follow our Facebook page.
